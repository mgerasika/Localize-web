How It Works

Localize-Web has two ways to translate your app.In first way app going to translate automaticaly, using google-translate-service,and all translations wiil save in our server.If you want something to change - you can use online editor.If you want to have all data on your server - just download it from localize-web. Second way - it's manual way - you press F2 on your site and translate what you want and all changes will saved on our server. By default you must enable this option in your account on localize-web.

So first steps is insert our javascript code into your web-site, and after that go to localize-web and register your site and manage what translation you want. If you want work offline without dependency to our servers,just download files from our server and insert it on your site. Also we planed WEB-HOOKs in future for automation this process.
